# Local SEO Blog Content - Local Guides

## Strategy

These articles serve two purposes:
1. Rank for informational and commercial keywords with local intent
2. Funnel readers toward service pages and city pages via internal links

Every article must end with a CTA to book a call. No fluff, no generic "digital is important" filler. Each piece must deliver concrete value so readers trust P-XEL before they even make contact.

## Content Rules for All Articles

1. No generic introductions ("In today's digital world..."). Start with the point.
2. Every article must have at least 2 internal links to service or city pages.
3. Every article must end with a Calendly CTA.
4. Use concrete numbers, real examples, specific names (programs, organizations, amounts).
5. Write in French, natural tone, premium but not pretentious.
6. Include FAQ schema for any question-answer sections.
7. Meta descriptions must include primary keyword and a value proposition.
8. Featured image: branded P-XEL visual, not stock photos.
