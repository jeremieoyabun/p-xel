# GBP Insights - Monthly Tracking and Reporting

## Key GBP Metrics to Track Monthly

Google Business Profile provides performance data through the "Insights" section (now called "Performance" in the new GBP dashboard). Track these metrics every month to measure local SEO progress.

### Core metrics

| Metric | What it tells you | Where to find it |
|---|---|---|
| Total searches | How often P-XEL appears in search results | Performance > Search queries |
| Direct searches | People who searched for "P-XEL" specifically | Performance > Search queries > Direct |
| Discovery searches | People who found P-XEL via category/service search | Performance > Search queries > Discovery |
| Branded searches | People who searched for the brand | Performance > Search queries > Branded |
| Website clicks | How many clicked through to p-xel.be | Performance > Customer actions |
| Direction requests | How many asked for directions (low for SAB) | Performance > Customer actions |
| Phone calls | How many called directly from GBP | Performance > Customer actions |
| Booking clicks | How many clicked the booking link (Calendly) | Performance > Customer actions |
| Photo views | How many viewed your photos | Performance > Photos |
| Total interactions | Sum of all customer actions | Performance > Overview |

## Search Queries Report

### How to read the search queries data

GBP shows which search terms triggered your listing to appear. This is gold for understanding how people find P-XEL.

### Query categories

**Direct searches:** User searched "P-XEL" or "P-XEL Studio." This indicates brand awareness. Growing direct searches means brand building is working.

**Discovery searches:** User searched "web designer Liege" or "creation site web" and found P-XEL. This is the primary metric for local SEO success. Growing discovery searches means rankings are improving.

**Branded searches:** User searched "P-XEL web design" or "P-XEL avis." Mix of brand and category. Growing branded searches means people know P-XEL and are evaluating it.

### What to look for each month

1. **Top discovery queries:** Which service/category terms are driving the most impressions? These indicate where P-XEL ranks well.
2. **Missing queries:** Which target keywords are NOT appearing? These indicate ranking gaps to work on.
3. **Query trends:** Are discovery searches increasing month over month? This is the primary growth indicator.
4. **New queries:** Any queries appearing for the first time? These may reveal new opportunities or changes in search behavior.

## Customer Actions

### Tracking customer actions

| Action | Target (Month 1-3) | Target (Month 4-6) | Target (Month 7-12) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Website clicks | 30/month | 60/month | 100+/month |
| Phone calls | 2/month | 5/month | 8+/month |
| Booking clicks | 5/month | 10/month | 20+/month |
| Direction requests | N/A (SAB) | N/A | N/A |

These targets assume a new or recently optimized GBP listing. Adjust based on actual baseline.

### Interpreting customer actions

- **High impressions, low clicks:** Listing appears but does not stand out. Improve: photos, reviews, business description, posts.
- **High clicks, low calls/bookings:** Website does not convert. Improve: landing page, CTA placement, Calendly integration.
- **Growing calls but no bookings:** Clients prefer calling over booking online. Ensure phone is answered promptly. Consider adding call tracking.
- **Booking clicks growing:** Calendly funnel is working. Monitor actual bookings in Calendly to verify.

## Photo Views and Engagement

### Photo strategy for GBP

Photos directly impact engagement. Listings with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more website clicks (Google data).

### Photo requirements

| Photo type | Quantity | Specifications |
|---|---|---|
| Logo | 1 | 720x720 px, transparent or solid background |
| Cover photo | 1 | 1200x900 px, best project or studio shot |
| Portfolio work | 8-12 | 1200x900 px, best projects, different service types |
| Team/workspace | 2-3 | 1200x900 px, professional but authentic |
| Process shots | 2-3 | 1200x900 px, design work in progress, whiteboard sessions |

### Photo optimization

- Use high-quality, professional images (no stock photos)
- Name files with keywords before uploading: "web-design-liege-pxel.jpg"
- Add photos monthly (at least 1 new photo per month) to signal activity
- Track photo views in GBP Performance. If views drop, add new photos.
- Remove any photos uploaded by others that are low quality or irrelevant

### Photo metrics to track

- Total photo views per month
- Photo views compared to competitors (GBP shows this comparison)
- Which photos get the most views (double down on that style)

## Post Performance Tracking

### What to track per post

| Metric | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| Views | GBP > Posts > Individual post stats |
| Clicks | GBP > Posts > Individual post stats |
| CTA button clicks | GBP > Posts > Individual post stats |

### Post performance benchmarks

For a small local studio, expect:
- Views per post: 50-200
- Clicks per post: 5-20
- CTA clicks per post: 2-10

### What to do with post data

- Posts with high views but low clicks: Improve CTA or visual
- Posts with high clicks: Replicate the format and topic
- Posts with low views: Check timing (post on Tuesday-Thursday mornings) or improve the image
- Track which post types perform best (project showcases vs tips vs offers)

|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | 5 | 5 (initial push from past clients) |
| Month 2 | 8 | 3 |
| Month 3 | 10 | 2 |
| Month 6 | 18 | 2/month average |
| Month 12 | 30 | 2/month average |

### Review quality analysis

Monthly, read all reviews and categorize:
- Which services are mentioned? (reinforces GBP relevance)
- Which cities are mentioned? (reinforces geographic relevance)
- What adjectives are used? (feeds into AI understanding of P-XEL)
- Any negative patterns? (address immediately)

## Competitive Benchmarking in GBP

### Competitors to monitor

Identify 5-7 competing studios in the Liege area that appear in the local pack. Track monthly:

| Competitor metric | How to check |
|---|---|
| Review count | View their GBP listing |
| Average rating | View their GBP listing |
| Review velocity | Note count monthly, calculate growth |
| Photo count | View their GBP listing |
| Post frequency | View their GBP listing |
| Categories | View their GBP listing |
| Services listed | View their GBP listing |

### Competitive gaps to exploit

- Competitor has fewer reviews? Accelerate review collection.
- Competitor has no posts? Post weekly to differentiate.
- Competitor has poor photos? Invest in professional portfolio shots.
- Competitor missing a service category? Add it to P-XEL's listing.
- Competitor has no Q&A? Seed P-XEL's Q&A section.
