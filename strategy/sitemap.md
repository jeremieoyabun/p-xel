## Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub-and-spoke model

```
Homepage
  -> Services hub -> 4 service pages
  -> Portfolio hub -> 6 case studies
  -> Blog index -> blog posts
  -> City pages (footer nav)

Service pages <-> relevant case studies
Service pages <-> relevant blog posts
Blog posts <-> related blog posts
Blog posts -> relevant service pages
City pages -> all service pages
Case studies -> relevant service page
All pages -> contact
```

### Link rules
1. Every service page links to at least 2 case studies and 2 blog posts
2. Every case study links to 1 service page and the next case study
3. Every blog post links to at least 1 service page and 2 other blog posts
4. Every city page links to all 4 service pages
5. Footer contains: services, projets, blog, contact, city pages, legal
6. Header nav: Services (dropdown), Projets, A propos, Contact

### Navigation structure

**Header:**
- Services (dropdown: 4 service pages)
- Projets
- A propos
- Contact (CTA button style)

**Footer:**
- Services: 4 service links
- Studio: A propos, Contact, Blog
- Villes: Liege, Bruxelles, Namur, Luxembourg
- Legal: Mentions legales, Politique de confidentialite
- Contact info: email, phone
- Social: LinkedIn, Facebook
