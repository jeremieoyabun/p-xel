# Phase 1 Sitemap - P-XEL Studio

The strongest possible V1. Not the biggest. Every page earns its place.

---

## Selection Principle

Phase 1 includes only pages that satisfy at least one of these criteria:

1. **Without it, the site cannot convert.** (homepage, contact)
2. **Without it, the site cannot prove capability.** (case studies)
3. **Without it, the site loses critical SEO ground.** (top service pages, Liege city page)
4. **Without it, the site feels incomplete to a qualified visitor.** (studio, work)
5. **It is legally required.** (legal pages)

If a page is "nice to have" or "will help eventually," it waits.
