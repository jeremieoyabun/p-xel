# Positioning - P-XEL Studio

## 1. Positioning Statement

P-XEL Studio is a premium product design partner based in Liege, Belgium. We design, build, and ship digital products that generate real business results. We combine strategic UI/UX, strong branding, and full-stack execution to take projects from concept to live product, fast. We integrate AI into our workflows and into the products we build. We work with startups, PMEs, and ambitious brands across Belgium, Luxembourg, and Europe.

**One-line version:**
P-XEL conçoit, construit et livre des produits digitaux qui convertissent.

**English equivalent:**
P-XEL designs, builds, and ships digital products that convert.

## 4. Value Proposition Angles (5)

### A. Speed to market
P-XEL livre des produits digitaux fonctionnels en semaines, pas en mois. MVP, landing page, site complet : on passe du brief au lancement sans friction.
- Proof: Hopscotch (51 pages en 24h), process en 4 etapes, estimation gratuite rapide
- For: Founders who need to launch fast, PMEs with time pressure

### B. Design that ships
P-XEL ne s'arrete pas a la maquette. On conçoit, on construit, on deploie. Le design est au service du produit final, pas d'une presentation Figma.
- Proof: Greenmood (plateforme B2B complete), Oyabun (ecosysteme Web3 fonctionnel)
- For: Clients tired of agencies that deliver PDFs but not products

### C. One partner, full scope
Un seul interlocuteur pour la strategie, le design, le branding et le developpement. Pas de sous-traitance. Pas de coordination entre 4 prestataires.
- Proof: Arduenna Gin (branding + e-commerce + 103 campagnes Ads), Kokoro (identite + site complet)
- For: PMEs and startups without internal design/dev teams

### D. Premium without the overhead
La qualite d'une agence senior sans la lourdeur. Pas de compte managers. Pas de couches intermediaires. Un studio avec 16 ans d'experience qui travaille directement avec les decideurs.
- Proof: 98% satisfaction client, 49+ projets, relation directe
- For: Decision-makers who want quality and clarity without agency bloat

### E. AI-native product thinking
P-XEL integre l'IA dans ses workflows et dans les produits qu'il conçoit. Configurateurs intelligents, automatisations CRM, generation de contenu, interfaces adaptatives.
- Proof: Service pillar AI, AI-powered configurators for Greenmood
- For: Companies looking to integrate AI into their digital products

## 5. Offer Pillars (4)

### Pillar 1: Web Apps & MVPs
Conception et developpement de produits digitaux fonctionnels. Applications web, plateformes SaaS, outils metier, MVPs pour validation marche.
- Deliverables: strategie produit, UX/UI, prototypage, developpement full-stack, deploiement
- Keywords: creation application web, developpement MVP, plateforme sur mesure
- Case: Greenmood (plateforme B2B), Oyabun (ecosysteme Web3)

### Pillar 2: Sites Web & Landing Pages
Sites vitrines performants, landing pages de conversion, sites e-commerce. Optimises pour le SEO, la vitesse et la generation de leads.
- Deliverables: strategie SEO, UX/UI, developpement, optimisation performance
- Keywords: creation site web, landing page, site vitrine professionnel, e-commerce
- Case: Arduenna Gin (e-commerce + resultats), Kokoro (site vitrine)

### Pillar 3: UI/UX Design
Design d'interfaces et d'experiences utilisateur. Design systems, prototypage haute fidelite, audit UX, refonte d'interfaces existantes.
- Deliverables: audit UX, wireframes, design system, maquettes Figma, prototypes interactifs
- Keywords: UX designer, UI design, design system, refonte UX
- Case: Greenmood (UX/UI plateforme complexe), tous les projets

### Pillar 4: Branding & Identite
Creation et evolution d'identites de marque. Logo, direction artistique, charte graphique, systemes visuels pour le digital et le print.
- Deliverables: logo, charte graphique, direction artistique, supports de marque
- Keywords: branding, identite visuelle, creation logo, direction artistique
- Case: Paris Crowdfunding (identite complete), Kokoro (branding + web)

**Note on AI:** AI is not a separate pillar. It is integrated transversally across all four pillars as a capability accelerator (AI-powered prototyping, intelligent interfaces, automated workflows, AI-generated content). This avoids positioning P-XEL as an "AI agency" while showing AI fluency.

## 6. Target Audience Segments (3)

### Segment A: Startup Founders & Product Leads
- Profile: Founder or CPO of a startup (seed to Series A), 1-20 people
- Location: Belgium, Luxembourg, Northern France
- Need: Launch an MVP or digital product fast, limited internal team
- Budget: 5,000 to 30,000 EUR
- Pain: Agencies are too slow and too expensive for early-stage. Freelancers are unreliable. They need a partner who understands product, not just design.
- Trigger: Fundraise complete, market validation needed, competitor pressure
- Decision: Speed, portfolio quality, product thinking, cost clarity

### Segment B: PME Directors & Marketing Leads
- Profile: Director or marketing manager of a PME (20-200 people)
- Location: Wallonia, Brussels, Luxembourg
- Need: New website, brand refresh, digital platform, better online presence
- Budget: 8,000 to 50,000 EUR
- Pain: Current site is outdated. No leads come from the website. Internal team can't handle design + dev. Previous agency disappointed.
- Trigger: Brand evolution, competitive pressure, digital transformation, subsidy eligibility (Cheques Entreprises, SME Package)
- Decision: Credibility, local presence, clear deliverables, references

### Segment C: Agencies & Studios (White-label / Overflow)
- Profile: Creative agency, marketing agency, or dev studio that needs senior design/UX capacity
- Location: Belgium, Luxembourg, France
- Need: Overflow capacity for UX/UI, design systems, pitch decks, branding projects
- Budget: Project-based, 3,000 to 15,000 EUR
- Pain: Internal team is at capacity. Need quality execution without onboarding. Need speed.
- Trigger: New pitch, client deadline, team gap
- Decision: Portfolio quality, reliability, speed, discretion

## 7. CTA Angles (3)

### CTA A: Direct booking (primary)
**"Reserver un appel"** / **"Discutons de votre projet"**
- Destination: Calendly 30-min slot
- Use: Homepage hero, service pages, case study pages, contact page
- Why: Lowest friction. Direct access to the decision-maker. Matches the "no overhead" positioning.

### CTA B: Project starter (secondary)
**"Lancer mon projet"** / **"Demarrer un projet"**
- Destination: Contact form with project type selector, budget range, timeline
- Use: Service pages, blog articles with commercial intent, city pages
- Why: Qualifies leads before the call. Shows professionalism. Captures context.

### CTA C: Contextual conversion (tertiary)
**"Voir le projet [Nom]"** / **"Decouvrir comment on a fait"**
- Destination: Case study page
- Use: Homepage portfolio section, service pages (relevant case), blog articles
- Why: Builds trust through proof before asking for a call. Lets the work speak.

## 8. Final Recommendation

### Headline
**On conçoit. On construit. On livre.**

### Subheadline
**Sites web, applications et identites de marque. Un seul studio. De la strategie au deploiement.**

### Why this combination
- "On conçoit. On construit. On livre." is the sharpest articulation of the positioning shift. Three verbs. Clear scope. No fluff. It directly addresses the gap identified in the audit: the current site says "we help brands" but doesn't communicate execution.
- The subheadline grounds it in concrete deliverables (sites, apps, brands) and the single-partner advantage.
- The rhythm is French-native, premium, and works at any display size.
- It avoids: generic "we help" language, English buzzwords, AI-first positioning that alienates non-tech buyers, cliche startup tone.

### Voice direction
- Use "on" (we, informal but professional) consistently across the site
- Reserve "vous" for the audience ("votre projet", "votre marque")
- No "tu" anywhere on the site
- Short sentences. Active verbs. No superlatives without proof.
- Show, don't tell: every claim backed by a case study, a stat, or a deliverable list

### Positioning hierarchy on homepage
1. **Hero:** Headline + subheadline + "Reserver un appel" CTA
2. **Proof bar:** 16+ ans / 49+ projets / 98% satisfaction
3. **Pillars:** 4 offer pillars with brief descriptions
4. **Portfolio:** 3-4 strongest cases with results
5. **Process:** 4 steps (keeps trust and reduces friction)
6. **Social proof:** Client logos + testimonials (when available)
7. **CTA:** "Lancer mon projet" with intake form
