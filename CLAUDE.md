# P-XEL Studio Rebuild

## Mission
Rebuild p-xel.be into a premium, SEO-first, lead-generation website focused on:
- web apps
- MVP design and development
- high-performance websites
- landing pages
- UI/UX
- branding
- AI-powered product workflows

## Positioning
P-XEL Studio is not just a design studio.
It is a premium product partner that designs and ships real digital products fast.

Core positioning:
AI Product Designer · From idea to shipped product, fast · UI/UX · Full-Stack · Brand

## Brand DNA to preserve
- Keep the current P-XEL logo
- Keep the violet / black / white identity
- Keep the sharp, geometric, futuristic atmosphere
- Keep a premium, minimal visual language
- Keep Calendly booking flow
- Keep relevant portfolio credibility

## Geographic priority
- Belgium first
- French-speaking market first
- Liège first
- Expandable to other cities later

## Main goals
1. Generate qualified leads
2. Rank for high-intent service searches
3. Build strong local SEO authority
4. Present a premium and distinctive brand
5. Show that P-XEL designs and ships, not just mocks up

## Important folders
- `/public/legacy-assets/` contains reusable assets from the old site
- `/audit/` contains extraction and audit work
- `/strategy/` contains positioning and sitemap
- `/brand/` contains art direction and design tokens
- `/seo/` contains general SEO strategy
- `/local-seo/` contains local SEO strategy and execution docs
- `/content/` contains written website content
- `/dev/` contains architecture and build planning
- `/tasks/todo.md` contains current tasks
- `/tasks/lessons.md` contains lessons from user corrections
- `/tasks/decisions.md` contains validated decisions

## Mandatory working rules
1. Audit before redesign
2. Reuse only relevant legacy assets
3. Avoid generic AI startup aesthetics
4. Prioritize clarity, conversion, performance, and SEO
5. Every substantial content piece must start with:
   - search intent
   - primary keyword
   - secondary keywords
   - outline
6. Update `/tasks/lessons.md` after every important correction
7. Update `/tasks/decisions.md` after every important validated direction
8. Do not create fluff content
9. Do not use long dashes
10. Write with a premium, sharp, clear tone

## Design constraints
- Avoid Inter, Arial, Roboto
- Avoid cliché purple SaaS gradients
- Avoid cookie-cutter startup layouts
- Use distinctive typography
- Use atmospheric, geometric, and premium visual systems
- Motion must feel intentional and refined

## SEO priorities
- Focus on transactional and commercial intent
- Local SEO is a major pillar, not a secondary layer
- Build service pages that can rank
- Build service + city clusters
- Improve internal linking
- Ensure schema coverage
- Prioritize pages that can drive booked calls

## Conversion priorities
- Every major page should push toward booking a call
- Show credibility fast
- Explain clearly what is delivered
- Show speed, execution, and product thinking
- Make the process feel easy and high-value
