# Perspectives - Article Audit

Audit of the 6 legacy articles from the previous P-XEL WordPress site.
Each article is evaluated on: topic relevance, SEO value, content quality, rewrite potential, and lead-generation fit.

---

## 1. Combien coute un site web en 2026

- **Source**: `combien-coute-un-site-web-en-2026.md`
- **Date**: Nov 2025
- **Primary keyword**: combien coute un site web
- **Search intent**: Commercial / Transactional
- **Hero image**: Article-large_Web-design.webp
- **Thumbnail**: None dedicated (use hero resize)

### Content summary
Pricing guide with 4 tiers: landing page (900-3500), vitrine (3500-10000), e-commerce (6000-30000), web app (20000+). Covers what affects pricing, includes CTA to P-XEL.

### Strengths
- High commercial intent keyword - people searching this are ready to buy
- Concrete price ranges give real value
- Natural conversion path to "get a quote"
- Good internal linking potential to service pages

### Weaknesses
- Price ranges need verification for 2026 accuracy
- Missing comparison with competitor pricing models (freelance vs agency vs studio)
- No mention of P-XEL's specific pricing positioning
- Could be more specific about Belgian/francophone market context

### Verdict: KEEP AND REWRITE
- Priority: 1 (highest)
- This is a money page. Strongest commercial intent of all articles.
- Rewrite to sharpen positioning, update prices, add P-XEL differentiation
- Add FAQ schema, comparison table, internal links to service pages

## 2. Aides a la digitalisation en Belgique

- **Source**: `aides-digitalisation.md`
- **Date**: Nov 2025
- **Primary keyword**: aides digitalisation belgique
- **Search intent**: Informational / Commercial
- **Hero image**: Article-large_Digitalisation.webp
- **Thumbnail**: Articles-Digitalisation.webp

### Content summary
Guide to Belgian subsidies: Prime Digitalisation Bruxelles (25-70% coverage), Cheque Maturite Numerique Wallonie (50% up to 5000). Explains eligibility, process, what's covered.

### Strengths
- Unique content angle - few competitors cover this well in French
- Directly relevant to P-XEL's Belgian market
- Strong lead-gen hook ("we help you apply for subsidies")
- Concrete financial incentive reduces buying friction
- Good for local SEO authority

### Weaknesses
- Subsidy amounts and conditions may have changed since Nov 2025 - needs verification
- Missing Flanders/German-speaking community programs
- Could include a step-by-step "how P-XEL helps you get the subsidy" section

### Verdict: KEEP AND REWRITE
- Priority: 2
- Unique competitive advantage content. No one else in the studio space covers this well.
- Verify all subsidy details are current for 2026
- Add conversion section: "P-XEL handles the subsidy application for you"
- Add structured data for FAQ

## 3. IA pour les designers : les vrais usages en 2026

- **Source**: `ia-pour-les-designers-les-vrais-usages-en-2026.md`
- **Date**: Oct 2025
- **Primary keyword**: ia pour les designers
- **Search intent**: Informational
- **Hero image**: Article-large_AI.webp
- **Thumbnail**: Articles-AI.webp

### Content summary
8 sections covering AI tools for designers: ideation, prototyping, asset generation, copywriting, testing, personalization, design systems, workflow automation.

### Strengths
- Positions P-XEL as AI-savvy studio (aligns with "AI Product Designer" positioning)
- Practical content, not theoretical
- Good topical authority builder
- Supports the studio's differentiation narrative

### Weaknesses
- "For designers" audience is peers, not clients - weaker lead-gen angle
- Needs reframing: why should a client care that P-XEL uses AI?
- Tool recommendations may be outdated by March 2026
- Some sections are thin

### Verdict: KEEP AND REWRITE (with angle change)
- Priority: 3
- Reframe from "AI for designers" to "How AI makes your project faster and better"
- Target the client, not the designer community
- New angle: "Comment l'IA accelere la creation de votre produit digital"
- Show P-XEL's AI workflow as a competitive advantage for the client

## 4. No-code, low-code ou full-code

- **Source**: `no-code.md`
- **Date**: Dec 2025
- **Primary keyword**: no-code vs code
- **Search intent**: Informational / Commercial
- **Hero image**: Article-large_Code.webp
- **Thumbnail**: Articles-No-code.webp

### Content summary
Comparison of no-code (Webflow, Bubble), low-code (Retool, Appsmith), and full-code approaches. Decision framework based on project complexity, budget, scalability needs.

### Strengths
- Decision-making content - catches prospects evaluating options
- Natural lead-gen: "not sure? Let P-XEL advise you"
- Good keyword cluster potential (no-code belgique, webflow vs code, etc.)
- P-XEL can position as the studio that knows when each approach fits

### Weaknesses
- Needs stronger opinion - currently too neutral
- Missing P-XEL's stance: when they recommend each approach
- Could include real project examples
- Tool landscape moves fast - some references may be stale

### Verdict: KEEP AND REWRITE
- Priority: 4
- Add clear P-XEL point of view on when to use what
- Include case examples (anonymized if needed)
- Position P-XEL as the expert who helps you choose
- Strong internal linking to web app and MVP service pages

## 5. Pourquoi un design system est essentiel en 2026

- **Source**: `pourquoi-un-design-system-est-essentiel-en-2026.md`
- **Date**: Oct 2025
- **Primary keyword**: design system
- **Search intent**: Informational
- **Hero image**: Article-large_design-system.webp
- **Thumbnail**: Articles-Design-system.webp

### Content summary
10 sections on design system benefits: consistency, speed, scalability, collaboration, accessibility, brand coherence, cost reduction, onboarding, testing, future-proofing.

### Strengths
- Shows P-XEL expertise in systematic design thinking
- Relevant to enterprise/scale-up prospects
- Good topical depth

### Weaknesses
- Very informational - weak lead-gen path
- Target audience (design-system buyers) is narrow
- 10 sections feels like padding - several overlap
- Keyword "design system" is dominated by English-language content

### Verdict: KEEP BUT CONDENSE

- Add concrete ROI examples
