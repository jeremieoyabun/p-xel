# Editorial Keyword Clusters - P-XEL Studio

## Purpose

Each cluster groups a primary keyword with its semantic field, related queries, and supporting long-tail variations. This prevents cannibalization, ensures comprehensive topic coverage, and maps exactly which page owns which queries.

## Cross-cluster linking rules

1. Every cluster article links to at least 1 service page (commercial intent destination)
2. Every cluster article links to at least 1 other cluster article (topical mesh)
3. Pricing clusters (1, 6, 7) always cross-link to each other
4. Decision clusters (3, 8) always cross-link to each other
5. Subsidy cluster (2) links to all pricing clusters
6. No cluster article links to more than 3 other articles (avoid link dilution)
7. Service pages link back to their most relevant cluster article in a contextual paragraph, not a generic "read more" link
