# Editorial Keyword Clusters - P-XEL Studio (v2)

## Purpose

Each cluster groups a primary keyword with its semantic field, related queries, and long-tail variations. One cluster = one article. One article = one cluster. No overlap. No cannibalization.

Clusters are split into Phase 1 (4 legacy rewrites) and Phase 2 (new high-value content). Phase 2 clusters outperform the cut legacy articles by 10-50x in estimated lead-generation value.

## Cross-cluster linking rules

1. Every Phase 1 article links to at least 1 service page
2. Every Phase 1 article links to at least 1 other Phase 1 article
3. Pricing clusters (1, 5, 6) always cross-link
4. Decision clusters (3, 7) always cross-link
5. Subsidy cluster (2) links to all pricing clusters
6. No article links to more than 3 other articles (avoid equity dilution)
7. Service pages link back to their most relevant article in a contextual paragraph

## Cluster dependency map

```
Cluster 1 (pricing) = PILLAR (highest traffic, most links)
  |
  |-- Cluster 2 (subsidies) feeds back ("reduce cost")
  |-- Cluster 3 (no-code) feeds back ("tech affects budget")

Cluster 7 (agence vs freelance, Phase 2) = STANDALONE (self-resolving)

```

The pricing article is the editorial hub. All roads lead to or from it.
