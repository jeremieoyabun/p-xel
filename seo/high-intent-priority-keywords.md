# High-Intent Priority Keywords - P-XEL Studio

## Selection criteria

A keyword makes this list only if it meets ALL three conditions:
1. The searcher is likely to become a lead (not just reading, comparing, or learning)
2. P-XEL can realistically rank for it within 6 months
3. The keyword has sufficient volume to justify dedicated optimization

Keywords are ranked by composite score: **conversion potential x reachable volume x competitive feasibility**.

---

## Tier S: Must-win keywords

These are non-negotiable. If P-XEL does not rank for these within 6 months, the SEO strategy has failed.

### 1. creation site web belgique
- **Page**: `/services/creation-site-web/`
- **Volume**: 200-400/month (BE), 8,000-15,000/month (francophone for "creation site web" without geo)
- **Intent**: Commercial - ready to hire
- **Competition in Belgium**: Medium. Most Belgian agencies target this poorly.
- **Why must-win**: This is the core service keyword. Every Belgian searching this is a potential client.
- **Current status**: Page exists, keyword assigned. Content written. Needs to be built and indexed.
- **Action**: Launch with Phase 1. Monitor ranking weekly.

### 2. agence web liege
- **Page**: `/liege/`
- **Volume**: 150-350/month (BE)
- **Intent**: Local + Commercial - looking for a provider in Liege
- **Competition**: Medium-Low. Few quality competitors in Liege specifically.
- **Why must-win**: Home city. P-XEL has the strongest local signals (address, phone, GBP). Should dominate this query.
- **Current status**: City page content written. Needs build, GBP optimization, citations.
- **Action**: Launch with Phase 1. Aggressive GBP and citation building.

### 3. combien coute un site web
- **Page**: `/perspectives/combien-coute-un-site-web`
- **Volume**: 5,000-10,000/month (all francophone markets)
- **Intent**: Commercial-informational - price research before buying
- **Competition**: Medium-High. Many articles exist, but most are shallow or outdated.
- **Why must-win**: Highest volume reachable keyword. A top-5 ranking means 500-1,500 monthly visits from people thinking about buying a website. The conversion path (article > pricing > CTA > Calendly) is direct.
- **Current status**: Legacy article exists. Needs heavy rewrite with updated prices, P-XEL positioning, FAQ schema.
- **Action**: Publish as first perspectives article. Update quarterly to maintain freshness signals.

### 4. agence digitale liege
- **Page**: `/` (Homepage)
- **Volume**: 100-250/month (BE)
- **Intent**: Commercial - looking for a digital partner
- **Competition**: Low-Medium. P-XEL has strong brand signals for Liege.
- **Why must-win**: Brand authority query. If P-XEL does not rank #1 for this in Liege, something is fundamentally wrong.
- **Current status**: Homepage exists but targets phantom keyword "studio digital Liege". Must shift.
- **Action**: Update homepage meta title and H1 to naturally include "agence digitale" and "Liege". Not forced - the current headline "On concoit. On construit. On livre." can stay, but meta title and description must carry the keyword.

## Tier A: High-priority keywords

Strong lead potential. Should rank within 6-9 months.

### 5. developpement application web belgique
- **Page**: `/services/application-web-mvp/`
- **Volume**: 50-150/month (BE), 1,000-2,000/month (francophone)
- **Intent**: Commercial - looking for a dev partner
- **Competition**: Medium. Fewer competitors target this specifically in Belgium.
- **Why high-priority**: Highest average project value (15K-60K+). Each lead from this keyword is worth significantly more than a site vitrine lead.
- **Action**: Launch with Phase 1.

### 6. aides digitalisation belgique
- **Page**: `/perspectives/aides-digitalisation-belgique`
- **Volume**: 300-600/month (BE)
- **Intent**: Informational-commercial - looking for subsidies to fund a project
- **Competition**: Low. Very few quality French-language pages cover Belgian digital subsidies well.
- **Why high-priority**: Unique content advantage. Searchers are pre-qualified (they have a project in mind and are looking for financial help to execute it). P-XEL's "we handle the subsidy application" angle converts directly.
- **Action**: Publish as second perspectives article. Verify all subsidy amounts are current for 2026. Add FAQ schema.

### 7. agence web bruxelles
- **Page**: `/bruxelles/`
- **Volume**: 200-400/month (BE)
- **Intent**: Local + Commercial
- **Competition**: High. Brussels is the most competitive Belgian city for web agencies.
- **Why high-priority**: Largest Belgian market. Even ranking #5-10 brings qualified traffic. P-XEL has no Brussels office so this is harder, but the city page + service quality can compensate.
- **Action**: Phase 2 page. Build after Liege city page proves the template works.

### 8. devis site web
- **Page**: `/contact/`
- **Volume**: 100-250/month (BE), 2,000-4,000/month (francophone)
- **Intent**: Transactional - ready to request a quote
- **Competition**: Low for contact pages. Most agencies do not optimize their contact page for this keyword.
- **Why high-priority**: People searching this are at the bottom of the funnel. They want to submit a request NOW.
- **Action**: Optimize contact page title tag to include "devis" naturally. Example: "Contact et devis | P-XEL Studio - Liege". Add "Demandez un devis gratuit" as H1 or prominent heading.

### 9. refonte site web
- **Page**: H2 section on `/services/creation-site-web/` (Phase 1), dedicated article (Phase 2)
- **Volume**: 200-400/month (BE), 3,000-5,000/month (francophone)
- **Intent**: Commercial - has a website, wants to rebuild it
- **Competition**: Medium-High for dedicated pages. But as an H2 on the service page, achievable.
- **Why high-priority**: Refonte clients are the most qualified leads. They already understand the value of a website, they have a budget, they know what they want improved. Average project value is higher than new site projects.
- **Action**: Ensure the creation-site-web page has a strong "Refonte de site web" H2 section. Plan a dedicated perspectives article for Phase 2: "Refonte de site web : quand, pourquoi, et combien ca coute."

### 10. UX UI designer belgique
- **Page**: `/services/ux-ui-design/`
- **Volume**: 50-100/month (BE)
- **Intent**: Commercial
- **Competition**: Low-Medium. Few Belgian designers optimize well for this.

## Conversion path mapping

Every high-intent keyword must have a clear path to a booked call:

```
Search query
  -> Landing page (service page, city page, or article)
    -> Mid-page CTA or contextual link
      -> Contact page or Calendly embed
        -> Booked discovery call

Maximum 2 clicks from landing to booking.
No dead ends. No pages without CTAs.
```
